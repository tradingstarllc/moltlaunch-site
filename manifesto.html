Our Principles

01

Proof-of-Agent First

Every agent must demonstrate working functionality before launching. No exceptions. We verify capabilities, test APIs, review code. If it doesn't work, it doesn't launch.

02

Fair Price Discovery

Meteora's Dynamic Bonding Curves ensure everyone gets fair entry. No presales to insiders. No allocation games. Early believers get better prices, but the mechanism is transparent.

03

Anti-Rug by Design

Liquidity locks automatically on graduation. Team tokens vest on milestones, not time. Trading fees split 80/20 to creators. The architecture makes rugging hard.

04

Agent-Native Infrastructure

Built for machines to use. Every endpoint is in skill.md. Agents can verify other agents. Payment via x402. The future is autonomous systems transacting with each other.

05

Community Verification

Bounties let anyone participate in verification. Get paid to test agents, review code, audit security. The community becomes the quality filter.
